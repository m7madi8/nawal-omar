What can I expect from the Dahab retreat?

This retreat is not an ordinary holiday.

It is a safe space for you to slow down, step away from daily pressure, and hear yourself again.

Over five days, we will work on:

  • 01

    Calming the nervous system and easing stress and pressure.

  • 02

    Returning to the body through yoga, breath, and conscious movement.

  • 03

    Building genuine connections with women who share your values and awareness.

  • 04

    Restoring energy and vitality away from the noise of everyday life.

  • 05

    Discovering new spaces of inner strength and confidence.

  • 06

    Creating memories and experiences that stay with you long after the retreat ends.

Why Dahab?

Because Dahab is different.

The sea, the mountains, the simplicity, the people, and the calm energy all create an ideal environment for healing, reflection, and renewal.

It is no coincidence that thousands of people around the world choose Dahab every year as a place to return to themselves.

Because it is built with great care.

Who is this retreat for?

  • For the woman who feels she needs a real rest.
  • For the woman who wants a new experience outside her comfort zone.
  • For the woman seeking balance between body, mind, and emotions.
  • For the woman who wants to meet inspiring women and a supportive community.

Who is this retreat not for?

  • For those looking only for a fast-paced tourist trip full of activities.
  • For those who do not wish to participate in a women-only group.
  • For those with a health condition that prevents physical activity without consulting their doctor.
  • For those expecting instant or magical results within a few days.

Frequently asked questions

Can I join if I am coming alone?

Absolutely. In fact, most participants in previous retreats joined alone. One of the most beautiful things that happens is women who start as strangers and leave as friends.

Do I need previous yoga experience?

Not at all. All sessions are suitable for beginners and advanced practitioners, with modifications for every level.

Can I share a room with a friend?

Yes, and this can be arranged in advance when registering.

What should I bring?

A full list of details and essentials will be sent to each participant before the retreat date.

Cancellation policy

  • The deposit is non-refundable after booking is confirmed.
  • If cancellation occurs up to 30 days before the retreat, the amount paid can be transferred to a future retreat or event.
  • If cancellation occurs less than 30 days before the retreat, no refund is possible due to prior commitments with the hotel and service providers.
  • If a replacement participant is found, the booking can be transferred to her after prior coordination.

Please register only when you are sure you can commit to participating.

In Nawal's words

"Sometimes all we need is to step away a little… to return to ourselves."

I am Nawal Omar — a nurse specializing in women's health, a yoga teacher, and a daughter of the Palestinian community in Israel. I carry a message that began long before any retreat, project, or journey.

Since the beginning of my nursing path, my goal has been to raise health awareness in the Arab community and be part of the real change women and families deserve. I believed knowledge is power, and that every woman deserves support, awareness, and tools for a healthier, more balanced life.

Later I found in yoga an additional language of giving — one that goes beyond words and helps women return to their bodies, their breath, and their inner voice. From there I began building safe spaces that unite movement, awareness, and physical and emotional wellbeing.

My life has held difficult and painful experiences, among the hardest the loss of my father — the person who taught me giving, responsibility, and belonging. These experiences did not change my message; they deepened it, and taught me that pain can become strength, and that we can turn our hardest chapters into a truer, deeper way of serving others.

In recent years I have walked alongside more than 2,000 women across the country through workshops, lectures, programs, and women's retreats. This Dahab retreat is the seventh international retreat I lead.

I believe one woman's success does not diminish another's, and that the strongest communities are built on cooperation, not competition. I always seek partnerships with inspiring women professionals, because true feminine power shows when we hold each other's hands and walk together.

It is an honor to share this journey with you. I hope it becomes more than travel or vacation — a space where we return to ourselves and discover how much strength, light, and life still lives within us.

Women's Dahab Retreat

19.8 – 23.8

A journey back to the body, the sea, and the strength within

In one of Sinai's most beautiful places, between mountains, sea, and calm energy, we meet for a deep women's retreat in Dahab — at Nour Boutique Hotel, a seaside boutique hotel with a quiet spirit, ideal for retreats, meditation, yoga, and return to self.

nourboutiquehotel.com ↗

The retreat is built as a complete journey: from earth… to water… to body… to senses… to heart.

Journey moments

Day 1 · 19.8

Arrival & Opening

10:00 — Arrival in Dahab. Rest, room check-in, quiet time to breathe and settle into the place.

15:00 — Opening Ceremony. Opening the retreat, setting the intention, ice-breaking, meeting the women, and creating a safe space to begin together.

19:00 — Dinner. After dinner: a light mountain evening, calm, connection, and immersion in Dahab's atmosphere.

Day 2 · 20.8

Earth Element · Grounding & Roots

07:30 — Morning yoga. Meditation, breathing techniques, and a yoga session focused on earth: stability, safety, roots, and return to the body.

09:00 — Breakfast.

10:00 — Trip to Blue Hole – Abu Galum – Blue Lagoon. A magical day between sea and mountains. Blue Hole is one of Dahab's most famous and beautiful areas — deep blue waters, coral reefs, and rare natural energy. Then Abu Galum and Blue Lagoon, among Sinai's calmest and most beautiful spots, ideal for stepping away from noise and returning to simplicity.

At Blue Lagoon we work with power cards: each woman chooses a card, a message, and an intention to carry for the rest of the journey.

19:00 — Dinner. Free time to walk, rest, or sit with the sea.

Day 3 · 21.8

Water Element · Liberation & Flow

07:30 — Morning yoga. Breathing techniques and yoga focused on water: flow, flexibility, emotions, and release from tension.

09:00 — Breakfast.

10:00 – 17:00 — The most important and beautiful day of the retreat. A special day for the body and nervous system: ice bath + sauna + lunch.

The ice bath is not just a powerful experience — it is deep training in breath, presence, and nervous system regulation. It helps the body release stress, build resilience, activate circulation, and bring a high sense of clarity and inner strength. With the sauna, the body moves between heat and cold, contraction and relaxation, challenge and rest.

This day will be one of the retreat's strongest moments — a day when we feel how intelligent, strong, and capable the body truly is.

19:00 — Dinner. Free time and rest.

Day 4 · 22.8

The Senses · Presence & Connection

07:30 — Morning yoga. Meditation and deep work on the senses: how we hear, feel, touch, breathe, and return to the present moment.

09:00 — Breakfast.

17:30 — Sound Healing + Acro Yoga. A deep sound healing session to calm the nervous system, followed by Acro Yoga to strengthen trust, play, communication, and support among the women.

20:00 — Closing Circle. Closing the retreat circle: sharing, gratitude, intentions for returning home, and a gentle farewell ritual.

Day 5 · 23.8

Departure

Breakfast, packing, farewell to Dahab, and return home with renewed energy.

Dahab is not just travel… Dahab is a space where we return to ourselves.

⭐️ The standout experience: Ice Bath & Sauna day ⭐️

A full day dedicated to the body and nervous system, including:

❄️ Ice bath 🔥 Sauna 🥗 Lunch

The ice bath is not just a physical challenge — it is a deep experience of learning to control the breath under stress, calming the nervous system, building inner strength, and growing self-confidence.

Many women discover through this experience that they are far stronger than they ever believed.

Between the heat of the sauna and the cold of the water, the body lives a unique experience that helps activate circulation, raise energy levels, and bring a deep sense of mental clarity.

For me, this day is the heart of the retreat — one of the most impactful and transformative days of the entire experience. 🤍
